About

A Trusted Partner
for Business Transitions

Steadfast Holdings, LLC is a Minnesota-based business acquisition and investment firm focused exclusively on small businesses within the essential services sector. We understand the decades of hard work, trust, and relationships that go into building a thriving trade business.

Our approach is relationship-first. We work closely with business owners to understand their goals — whether that's a clean exit, a gradual transition, or continued involvement — and we structure every deal accordingly.

We are committed to preserving what makes each business great: its people, its reputation, and its community relationships.

Integrity

Transparent dealings, fair valuations, and honest communication at every stage of the acquisition process.

Stewardship

We acquire businesses to grow them — not strip them. Employees and customers remain our priority post-acquisition.

Long-Term Vision

We invest with a multi-decade horizon, focusing on sustainable growth over short-term financial engineering.

Local Roots

Proudly headquartered in Minnesota, we understand the regional market and are committed to the communities we serve.

Our Process

A Straightforward
Acquisition Process

01

Initial Conversation

We start with a confidential, no-obligation discussion to understand your business, your goals, and your timeline. No pressure — just honest dialogue.

02

Preliminary Valuation

We review basic financials and provide an indicative offer range quickly — typically within one to two weeks — so you know where things stand early.

03

Due Diligence

A collaborative, respectful review of your operations, financials, and contracts. We move efficiently and respect your time and confidentiality.

04

Offer & Close

We structure deals that work for you — including seller financing, earnouts, or continued involvement — and close on agreed timelines.

05

Transition Support

We work alongside you during transition to preserve customer relationships, support your team, and ensure business continuity.

Typical Acquisition Criteria

  • Minnesota-based service businesses
  • $500K – $10M in annual revenue
  • Established customer base and recurring work
  • 2+ years of operating history
  • Owner looking to retire, transition, or exit
  • Strong reputation in local market
  • Licensed and insured operations
