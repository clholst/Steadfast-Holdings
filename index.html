My Process

How I Work with
Sellers

01

A Real Conversation First

I start with a confidential, no-pressure call. I want to understand your business, your timeline, and what a good outcome looks like for you — before we ever talk numbers.

02

Respectful Due Diligence

I move quickly and keep things confidential. I respect your time and your business, and I won't drag out the process or make it harder than it needs to be.

03

A Deal That Works for You

I'm flexible on structure — seller financing, earnouts, phased transitions. I want to close a deal that genuinely works for both of us, not just get it done.

04

I'll Be There for the Handoff

I'll work alongside you through the transition to protect customer relationships and support your team. You won't feel like you just dropped the keys and walked away.

What I'm Typically Looking For

  • Minnesota-based service businesses

  • Established team and recurring customer base
  • 2+ years of operating history
  • Owner ready to retire, transition, or step back
  • Strong local reputation
